Our Commitment

We are committed to providing fair, unbiased career guidance to every user. Our AI models are trained to focus solely on skills and capabilities, ensuring that recommendations are based on merit and potential, not personal characteristics.

Users can edit their profiles anytime, regenerate recommendations, and have full control over their data. We believe in transparency and will never use sensitive attributes to influence career guidance.
